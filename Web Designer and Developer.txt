Position: Web Designer/Developer

Location: Los Angeles, California

Company Overview:
We are a rapidly growing company in the furniture and home lifestyle space. With a focus on expanding our digital presence, we’re looking to enhance our ecommerce existing platforms and pursue new opportunities in the Direct to Consumer (D2C) space as well. Our brand is known for creating unique product, and we need a dynamic individual to help bring our vision to life online.

Role Overview:
As a Web Designer/Developer, you will be responsible for developing and maintaining our ecommerce websites, particularly on Shopify, and supporting new D2C projects. This role demands a mix of technical development expertise and strong graphic design skills to create visually stunning, user-friendly digital experiences that reflect our brand's aesthetic. In addition to web development, you will collaborate across departments to contribute to social media strategies and other digital marketing efforts, ensuring a cohesive brand presence.

Key Responsibilities:

Lead the development and optimization of our Shopify ecommerce platforms.
Design and implement new website features, updates, and integrations to enhance customer experience.
Execute D2C initiatives from concept through to live implementation, ensuring alignment with brand vision.
Collaborate closely with marketing, design, and operations teams to ensure a cohesive brand experience across digital platforms.
Translate business needs into functional, well-designed digital assets, from website layouts to graphic elements.
Manage website performance, troubleshoot issues, and implement SEO best practices to drive traffic and conversions.
Support social media initiatives with design and digital assets to enhance brand presence.
Stay ahead of industry trends, tools, and best practices to ensure our digital offerings remain competitive and innovative.
Adapt to the evolving needs of the company, assisting in various functions as we grow and expand.
Key Qualifications:

Proficiency in Shopify development, with experience in customizing themes, building plugins, and integrating third-party applications.
Strong graphic design skills with a keen eye for detail and aesthetics (proficient in tools like Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, etc.).
Proven track record in ecommerce development, specifically in the home, lifestyle, or furniture industries.
Familiarity with UX/UI best practices and responsive design principles.
Ability to work cross-functionally with marketing, design, and operations teams.
Experience with SEO, Google Analytics, and performance optimization tools.
Strong project management skills with the ability to pivot and adapt in a high-growth environment.
Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.
A passion for the home and lifestyle space, with a desire to bring innovative ideas to life.
We are a dynamic and fast paced company, changing and growing quickly. We are looking for someone who can join our team in the same capacity and is comfortable with taking a dynamic and entrepreneurial approach to the day to day.
